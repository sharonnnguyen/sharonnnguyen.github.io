Art Painting Year 13

For my final college Art Painting submission, I've decided to create this 3 panelboard portfolio going back in time to discover deeper into my culture roots. Through these boards, I explored Vietnamese fairy tales from thousands of years back, to the wars we had to fight and learn more about the celebrations we enjoy as a country. This was very special to me as I felt like I have understood more about my homeland and realized how far we have come.

212.157 Dress - Assessment 1

My tea cup dress was inspired through a conversation I had with my partner Millie as she told me her favorite color is silver and that the reason behind this is because she’s always been fascinated with the silverware she has seen at her nonna’s house while growing up. Her family have been collecting silverware for the last 4 generations with items like silver tea cups, tea pots to spoons, jugs and many more. I could feel the connection she had towards these antique silverware and felt really inspired to create an outcome based on this connection of hers.
